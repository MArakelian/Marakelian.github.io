Hans Kelsen and the Pure Theory of Law

Jun 17 2021 07:00 PM EDT
Philosophy of Law , Legal Positivism

On our next stream we’ll be covering Hans Kelsen’s Pure Theory of Law. Specifically, We’ll be discussing his views on the Basic Norm (the Grundnorm), which is the rule that forms the underlying basis for the unified nature of a legal system.

Reading for this stream is: Hans Kelsen, A Pure Theory of Law (trans. by Max Knight, University of California Press, 1967) pp. 193-195, 198-201, and 208-14.
